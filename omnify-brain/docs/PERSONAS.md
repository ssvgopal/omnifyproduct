# Omnify Brain - Target Personas

## Strategic Alignment (Research Brief V3)

**Target Market**: DTC/Subscription brands in Beauty, Skincare, Supplements, Wellness
**Revenue Range**: $50M - $350M annual revenue
**Value Proposition**: "20-40% waste reduction" through intelligent marketing spend optimization

## üéØ Primary Personas

### 1. Sarah Martinez - CMO
**Decision Authority**: 10/10 (Highest budget authority)

**Profile**:
- **Title**: Chief Marketing Officer
- **Company**: $285M Beauty Subscription brand (DTC)
- **Team Size**: 45 people across brand, growth, and creative
- **Ad Spend**: $8M/year across Meta, Google, TikTok

**Pain Points**:
- Opens six conflicting dashboards every morning
- CEO asks "Why did CAC jump?" and she can't answer in 30 seconds
- Spends 3 hours/week reconciling platform data
- Doesn't trust any single source of truth

**Needs from Omnify**:
- **One screen, one story** - CEO-ready insights
- **LTV-ROAS focus** - Not just immediate returns
- **Executive summary** - "Here's the truth" format
- **What will break if you don't act** - Risk-focused messaging

**UI Preferences**:
- High-level metrics (MER, Blended ROAS, LTV-ROAS)
- Strategic impact ($) over tactical details
- Simplified language, no jargon
- "Here's exactly where to move budget" recommendations

---

### 2. Jason Li - VP Growth
**Decision Authority**: 9/10 (Principal martech decision-maker)

**Profile**:
- **Title**: VP of Growth
- **Company**: $220M Cosmetics DTC/Hybrid brand
- **Team Size**: 12 performance marketers
- **Ad Spend**: $6M/year, manages Meta/Google/TikTok spend directly

**Pain Points**:
- Refreshes Meta Ads Manager every 15 minutes
- Pulse of anxiety when CAC spikes unexpectedly
- Needs to justify spend to CMO weekly
- Creative fatigue catches him off guard

**Needs from Omnify**:
- **Fatigue alerts** - "Creative C12 will fatigue in 3 days"
- **Budget recommendations** - "Shift $12K from TikTok ‚Üí Meta"
- **Confidence scores** - Quantified certainty on predictions
- **LTV drift warnings** - "TikTok cohort LTV dropped 8%"

**UI Preferences**:
- Channel comparison views
- ROAS trends with time-series
- Detailed rationale for recommendations
- Confidence percentages on all predictions

---

### 3. Emily Chen - Director of Performance Marketing
**Decision Authority**: 7/10 (Day-to-day execution authority)

**Profile**:
- **Title**: Director of Performance Marketing
- **Company**: $140M Hybrid brand (DTC + Retail)
- **Team Size**: 6 media buyers
- **Ad Spend**: $4M/year, executes campaigns daily

**Pain Points**:
- Needs tactical flags, not strategy documents
- Drowning in data but starving for insights
- Spends 2 hours/day pulling reports
- Misses optimization windows due to delayed data

**Needs from Omnify**:
- **Action-first format** - "Pause Creative C12 now"
- **Exact actions** - "Move 10% from TikTok to Meta"
- **Tactical execution details** - Creative IDs, exact amounts
- **Quick scannability** - No reading required

**UI Preferences**:
- Action-first, explanation second
- Tactical numbers (spend amounts, percentages)
- Creative IDs and campaign names
- Urgency indicators (NOW, TODAY, THIS WEEK)
