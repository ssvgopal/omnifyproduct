# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated**: January 2025  
**Version**: 1.0

---

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

---

## 2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

- **Authentication**: Keep you logged in
- **Preferences**: Remember your settings
- **Analytics**: Understand how you use our Service
- **Security**: Detect and prevent fraud
- **Functionality**: Enable features and improve performance

---

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Essential Cookies

**Required for the Service to function**

- Session management
- Authentication tokens
- Security features

**Cannot be disabled**

### 3.2 Analytics Cookies

**Help us understand usage**

- Google Analytics
- Page views, user flows
- Feature usage statistics

**Can be disabled** (see Section 5)

### 3.3 Functional Cookies

**Remember your preferences**

- Language settings
- Theme preferences
- Dashboard layouts

**Can be disabled** (may affect functionality)

---

## 4. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may set cookies:

- **Google Analytics**: Website analytics
- **Sentry**: Error tracking
- **Stripe**: Payment processing
- **Intercom**: Customer support (if enabled)

These services have their own privacy policies.

---

## 5. Managing Cookies

### 5.1 Browser Settings

You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome**: Settings > Privacy > Cookies
- **Firefox**: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
- **Safari**: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
- **Edge**: Settings > Privacy > Cookies

### 5.2 Our Cookie Consent

- We show a cookie consent banner on first visit
- You can accept or decline non-essential cookies
- Your preference is saved in your browser

### 5.3 Opt-Out Links

- **Google Analytics**: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

---

## 6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling cookies may:

- Prevent you from logging in
- Limit functionality
- Affect your user experience

Essential cookies are required for the Service to work.
